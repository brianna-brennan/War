THIS IS WAR!

You will be competing against the computer in a game of luck. The deck is divided evenly, with each player receiving 26 cards. To start the game, click on the “Draw Card” button. A card will be drawn for each player at the same time and the player with the higher card gets a point. If the cards are the same rank, its War and neither player gets a point. The first player to 10 points wins….may the odds be ever in your favor!
